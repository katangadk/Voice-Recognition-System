Technical Audit Report

SEC2201 - Biometrics 1

01. Strategic Objective

ECHOSECURE is engineered as a benchmark for high-assurance biometric authentication. By utilizing Mel-Frequency Cepstral Coefficients (MFCC), the system bypasses traditional vulnerabilities of knowledge-based security, providing an immutable link between the biological user and their digital access.


ECHOSECURE is a fully functional, professional-grade voice biometric authentication prototype developed as part of the Biometric Systems course (SEC2201) at the School of ICT, Zambia University College of Technology.

Encryption Architecture

Templates are hashed using AES-256-GCM with unique salts, ensuring that even in a breach, raw biometric data remains unintelligible.
